Frequently Asked Questions

What are your gym timings?

We're open 5 AM – 11 AM and 5 PM – 10 PM, Monday through Saturday. Sunday is generally a holiday, with limited access for Premium members on request.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes — you can book a free trial session using the "Book Free Trial" button and our team will get in touch to schedule your visit.

Is personal training included in membership?

Personal training and diet guidance are included in our Premium plan. Basic and Standard members can add personal training sessions separately.

Do you have a dedicated space for women?

Yes, our Women Fitness program includes dedicated coaching and training slots designed specifically for our women members.

Where exactly are you located?

We're on Green Hills Colony Road No. 3, Dwarka Nagar, Kothapet, Hyderabad, Telangana 500102 — see the map in our Contact section for directions.
